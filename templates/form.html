About

In this project we extract tweets based on demography, time-duration, and relevance of the tweets based on its favourite and retweet count. We find trending hashtags similar to the topic of interest and then after processing it we apply machine learing based intelligent cognitive solution provided by IBM Watson Alchemy API to judge the actual sentiment people tried to convey while writing the tweets.

Tweestat is a portmanteau of Twitter and Statistics. It aims to provide professionals and companies in both the public and private sectors useful information about public opinions and sentiments on topics relevant to them, which in turn will enable them to make an informed decisions. For example, a TV production house might use this to judge the public response to an episode of a show, and improve the show accordingly.
